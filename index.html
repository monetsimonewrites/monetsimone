The Author

Hi, I'm Monet Simone.

I write sports romance for readers who want it all — the butterflies, the banter, the big feelings, and the moments that make you put the book down just to collect yourself.

The Starting Lineup Series was born from my love of competition, chemistry, and characters who push each other to be better — on the court and off it.

When I'm not writing, I'm probably watching basketball, plotting the next book in the series, and enjoying time with my family.

Sports Romance ✦ Coming July 2026

The Long Game

The Long Game is a friends-to-lovers romance about Cairo Clayton, a guarded basketball coach carrying painful secrets, and Desmond "DC" Montague, a retired NBA superstar forced to confront the life and love he walked away from. What begins as a fake relationship to protect his image soon becomes impossible to fake as years of tension, loyalty, and unresolved feelings rise to the surface. ❤️‍🔥
